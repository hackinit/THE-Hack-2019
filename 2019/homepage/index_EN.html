Hi, Welcome to THE Hack 2019！

“THE”, a word that is homophonic to “virtue” in Chinese, represents our mission to invite more young, aspiring and talented youth to to apply their technical knowledge and skills, combined with their unique observation about the world, to solve issues of various kinds ranging from lifestyle to possibly, human future.

This year, we are focusing on “Technology Harvests Enlightenment”. THE Hack seeks to support the young generation to use technology as a mean to harvest human enlightenment, finding feasible solutions in various industries including education, healthcare, finance and more.

Similar to THE Hack 2018, THE Hack 2019 will provide sophisticated participants with resources and bridges to high-end technology as well as bringing knowledge and platforms for practice to novices. At the meantime, THE Hack 2019 will incorporate the two groups of last year, invite 300 hackers to participate, and create better develop experience for them.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is THE Hack?
THE Hack is China's premier youth hackathon and the first hackathon that promotes inclusivity and cultural diversity. We are bringing together 300 brightest minds across the country to Shanghai for a 24-hour, nonstop feast of creativity, collaboration, and rapid learning.
How much will this cost?
Nada. Zip. Zero. Zilch. We'll provide you with a weekend's worth of swag, meals, drinks, and snacks and a place to crash when you need a break from coding. In fact, we'll even reimburse your travel.
Who can participate?
Anyone are welcome to participate, whether you are currently enrolled in high school, university or dropped out. For those who are over the age limit, you are still encouraged to apply to be considered for our Invitational that runs simultaneously with our main event. Our VC and incubator partners will pay more attention to your work!
What if I am under 16 and I want to come?
Unfortunately, due to legal restrictions, we are unable to host minors less than 16 years old. However, feel free to come to our expo on July 13 to check out awesome projects built by others during the hackathon.
I've never been to a hackathon and I'm nervous.
Have no fear - THE Hack is, first and foremost, a fun learning experience! We will have beginner workshops held by industry professionals in design, mobile applications, web, hardware, AI, VR and more! Once you are accepted, you will have the opportunity to come to some workshops before and at the hackathon to better prepare yourself.
How will I get to THE Hack?
For Shanghai hackers, you can get to Shanghai Jiao Tong University easily by public transportation which will not be reimbursed. We do provide travel reimbursement to non-Shanghai hackers. More information will be available to you once accepted.
What's the event schedule?
THE Hack will commence at 8 am, July 13 and last until 5 pm, July 14. More detailed schedule will be available closer to event date.
Can I sleep, workout...?
Of course! We will have designated resting area during our event. You can also enjoy the clean air in Shanghai Jiao Tong University by jogging around the campus!
What should I bring?
Bring your government-issued ID for entrance and to borrow hardware from our rental booth. You’ll also need your laptop and charger for hacking, a blanket or a sleeping bag for resting, and toiletries for hygiene.
I still have more questions!
No problem! Email us anytime at marketing@hackinit.org.
